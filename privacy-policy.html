Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2025 | Effective Date: January 30, 2025

1. Introduction and Scope

Welcome to Luna Launch ("Platform", "we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our Solana token creation and deployment platform.

By accessing or using our Platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not use our Platform.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of our Platform and covers both personal information and blockchain-related data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Token Creation Data: Token names, symbols, descriptions, supply amounts, and metadata
  • Upload Content: Logos, images, and other media files for token branding
  • Social Media Links: Telegram, Twitter/X, and website URLs when provided
  • Support Communications: Messages sent through our support channels
  • Survey Responses: Feedback and survey responses when voluntarily provided

2.2 Blockchain and Wallet Information

  • Wallet Addresses: Public Solana wallet addresses used to interact with our Platform
  • Transaction Data: Transaction hashes, amounts, and timestamps from blockchain interactions
  • Token Contract Addresses: Addresses of tokens created through our Platform
  • Liquidity Pool Data: Information related to liquidity pools created or managed

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Analytics: Platform interactions, feature usage, and user flow patterns
  • Technical Information: IP addresses, browser types, device information, and operating systems
  • Performance Data: Page load times, error logs, and system performance metrics
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session cookies, preference settings, and analytics cookies

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Platform Operations

  • Processing token creation and deployment requests
  • Managing liquidity pools and associated transactions
  • Facilitating wallet connections and blockchain interactions
  • Providing customer support and technical assistance

3.2 Platform Improvement and Analytics

  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve Platform functionality
  • Identifying and fixing technical issues and bugs
  • Developing new features and services
  • Conducting research and analytics for business insights

3.3 Legal and Compliance

  • Complying with applicable laws and regulations
  • Responding to legal requests and law enforcement inquiries
  • Preventing fraud, money laundering, and other illegal activities
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service and protecting user safety

3.4 Communications

  • Sending platform updates and important notifications
  • Providing customer support responses
  • Sharing security alerts and system maintenance notices

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contractual Necessity: Processing required to provide our Platform services
  • Legitimate Interest: Platform improvement, security, and business operations
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with applicable laws and regulations
  • Consent: Where explicitly provided for specific processing activities

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We DO NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

5.2 We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Blockchain Networks: Token and transaction data is publicly visible on the Solana blockchain
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate the Platform (analytics, hosting, support)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court orders, or government authorities
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of business assets
  • Safety and Security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of users and the public

5.3 Third-Party Integrations

Our Platform integrates with various third-party services:

  • Wallet Providers: Phantom, Solflare, and other Solana wallets
  • Analytics Services: Google Analytics and similar services for usage analytics
  • Support Services: Customer support platforms and live chat services
  • Blockchain Services: Solana RPC endpoints and blockchain data providers

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information for different periods based on the type of data:

  • Token Creation Data: Retained indefinitely as required for Platform functionality
  • Usage Analytics: Typically retained for 24-36 months
  • Support Communications: Retained for 3 years after resolution
  • Legal Compliance Data: Retained as required by applicable laws

Note: Blockchain data is permanently recorded and cannot be deleted due to the immutable nature of blockchain technology.

7. Data Security and Protection

7.1 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption for data transmission and storage
  • Regular security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure hosting infrastructure with reputable cloud providers
  • Regular security updates and patch management

7.2 User Responsibilities

  • Maintaining the security of your wallet and private keys
  • Using secure networks when accessing the Platform
  • Reporting suspected security incidents promptly
  • Keeping your devices and browsers updated

7.3 Incident Response

In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by applicable laws, typically within 72 hours of discovery.

8. Your Privacy Rights

8.1 General Rights (All Users)

  • Access: Request information about the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal and technical limitations)
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing

8.2 Additional Rights for EEA Users (GDPR)

  • Data Portability: Receive your personal data in a machine-readable format
  • Restrict Processing: Request limitation of data processing in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for processing based on consent
  • Lodge Complaints: File complaints with supervisory authorities

8.3 Additional Rights for California Users (CCPA)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected, used, and shared
  • Right to delete personal information (subject to exceptions)
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

To exercise your rights, contact us through our support channels. We will respond to valid requests within 30 days.

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

9.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for Platform functionality and security
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand Platform usage and performance
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

9.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect Platform functionality.

9.3 Analytics and Third-Party Tracking

We use Google Analytics and similar services to understand Platform usage. These services may collect information across multiple websites and platforms.

10. International Data Transfers

Your personal information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws than your country.

When we transfer personal data internationally, we implement appropriate safeguards such as:

  • Standard contractual clauses approved by relevant authorities
  • Adequacy decisions by competent authorities
  • Appropriate technical and organizational security measures

11. Children's Privacy

Our Platform is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will delete such information immediately. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

12. Blockchain-Specific Privacy Considerations

Important Notice: Blockchain technology has unique privacy implications:

  • All blockchain transactions are publicly visible and permanent
  • Wallet addresses and transaction history cannot be deleted from the blockchain
  • Token metadata may be publicly accessible through blockchain explorers
  • Smart contract interactions are permanently recorded on the blockchain

While we can delete personal information from our systems, blockchain-recorded data remains permanently visible on the public Solana network.

13. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors.

We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated Privacy Policy on our Platform
  • Displaying prominent notices on the Platform
  • Sending notifications through available communication channels

Your continued use of the Platform after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

14. Contact Information and Data Protection Officer

For privacy-related questions, requests, or concerns, please contact us through:

  • Our support channels available on the Platform
  • Discord support server: https://discord.gg/lunalaunch
  • Direct message through our official social media channels

For EEA users, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority if you believe your personal data has been processed unlawfully.

Response Time: We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days and will fulfill valid requests within 30 days as required by applicable law.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date last updated and supersedes all prior privacy policies relating to the subject matter herein.
